Ingredients
	- 8-oz. elbow macaroni pasta
	- 1 c. extra-sharp cheddar cheese (shredded)
	- 2 c. milk
	- 2 tbsp. butter
	- 2 tbsp. flour
	- 1/2 tsp. salt
	- 1/4 tsp. black pepper
	- 1/4 tsp. red pepper (optional)

Directions
	1. Preheat oven to 400°. 
	2. Microwave 2 cups milk at HIGH for 1 1/2 minutes. 
	3. Melt 2 Tbsp. butter in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-low heat; whisk in 2 Tbsp. all-
	   purpose flour until smooth. Cook, whisking constantly, 1 minute.
	4. Gradually whisk in warm milk, and cook, whisking constantly, 5 minutes or until thickened.
	5. Whisk in 1/2 tsp. salt, 1/4 tsp. black pepper, 1 cup shredded extra-sharp Cheddar cheese, and, 
	   if desired, 1/4 tsp. red pepper until smooth.
	6. Stir in 1/2 (16-oz.) package elbow macaroni pasta. 
	7. Spoon pasta mixture into a lightly greased 2-qt. baking dish; top with extra-sharp Cheddar 
	   cheese. 
	8. Bake at 400° for 20 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
